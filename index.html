The Who?

My name is Katherine Denney. And welcome to my little corner of the internet!

This page will be my page to fiddle with building code (don't be too harsh, I built this from scratch while learning github/html/css) and to showcase many of my projects and work experience. If you need something specific, go straight down to the footer and feel free to email me.
